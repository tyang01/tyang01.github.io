SOMEWHERE BETWEEN THE US AND KOREA

After living in Korea for 26 years, and by chance, I start to study abroad in Baltimore, US. Culture conflict is continuing till now from then.

In Korea, we have to ask age first when meeting someone, and have to control our languages according to the age I recognized.

'Current Situation' matters much more than 'my opinion', in Korea. So I have to edit what I want to say in this situation.

Korea is adjective-and-noun-oriented language while English is verb-oriented one. So it is little bit hard to naturally translate.
