Shoyou restaurant first opened its doors on Light Street in Baltimore, MD in 2000. At that time there were only 28 seats, no liquor license and no menu. However, it was Baltimore's first late night Japanese restaurant and was blessed with success almost instantly. Chef Fujiwara maintains a high standard of fish selection, hand picking the freshest fish for his restaurant.

Shoyou Sushi is one of the most coveted sushi spots in Baltimore. Although the restaurant size is small, the sushi rolls are truly larger than life. Chef Fujiwara maintains a high standards of fish selection, hand picking the freshest fish for his restaurant.

He works alongside a team of a few hard working employees, who constantly work twice as hard to make up for the small team. SHoyou Sushi brings in a variety of customers from the DC/Baltimore area, and maintains its reputation for the freshest and premium fish in the area.
