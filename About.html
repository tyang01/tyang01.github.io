Our History

FOUNDED IN 2000

Shoyu restaurant first opened its doors on Light Street in Baltimore, MD in 2000. At that time there were only 28 seats, no liquor license and no menu. However it was baltimore's first late night Japanese restaurant and was blessed with success almost instantly. Chef Fujiwara maintains a high standard of fish selection, hand picking the freshest fish for his restaurant.

Nothing can be done without hope and confidence. If you can dream it, you can do it. Do it now, not tomorrow. Always continue the climb.
